What is the Challenge?

  1. Turn your phone on silent, then set a timer (you choose for how long) and put the phone face down. Encourage your dinner companions to do the same.
  2. Now the fun part: connect with loved ones, savor your food, and create new memories. Enjoy the experience.
  3. So, how was it? Use DinnerMode on social media to describe or show your experience.

Think you can get others to take the challenge, too? Spread the word about DinnerMode!

The Inspiration

DinnerMode is the inspiration of Sloane Davidson, an activist, digital communications professional, fundraiser and burgeoning farmer. She was inspired to start this movement in the summer of 2014 when she left technology behind to go volunteer on a farm in upstate New York following the sudden and tragic death of her best friend. She had an aha moment when she realized that we're becoming increasingly addicted to our devices and not being mindful of the present moment.

DinnerMode launched on the BOLD stage at INBOUND14 in September, 2014. Since launching, people all over the country have taken the DinnerMode challenge. Keep visiting DinnerMode.org this fall to learn more and find new ways to get involved.

Sloane would like to thank everyone who helped her launch this movement, especially Gerard Ramos and the team at Revelry.
